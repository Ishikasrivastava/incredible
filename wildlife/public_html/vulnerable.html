Some of the Vulnerable Species

Yak

Yak is one of the largest species of wild bovid found throughout the Himalaya region of India and well adapted to high altitudes. Wild yaks are among the largest bovids and also the largest native animal in their range.


Nilgiri

Nilgiri Marten is the only species of marten found in India and one of the most rare and amazing wild animal endemic to the Western Ghats of India. It occurs in the hills of the Nilgiris and parts of southern Kodagu.


Marbled Cat

Marbled Cat is one of the small wild cat species listed as vulnerable, found along the Himalayan foothills in India. The marbled cat is famous for its arboreal lifestyle and its prey includes birds, squirrels,rodents and reptiles.


Smooth-coated Otters

Smooth-coated otters occur throughout much of the Indian Subcontinent, mostly found in rivers,wetlands and seasonal swamps. Because of the loss of wetland habitats,reduction in prey and due to poaching, Smooth coated otter has been categorised as vulnerable species .
